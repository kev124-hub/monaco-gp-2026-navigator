02 of 05
Race-Hours Routing
Thu–Sun (~05:00–21:00), the circuit blocks direct routes. The app automatically detours you around it. Want to pre-plan before you arrive? Tap ⏰ Simulate Race Hours in the Directions panel — it forces race-closed routing so you can map your route in advance.
03 of 05
Get Step-by-Step Directions
Tap Directions & Layers at the bottom. Tap 📍 to use GPS as your start, or type any hotel, gate, or landmark. Add a destination, then tap 🚶 Get Walking Directions. Circuit closures are handled automatically.
04 of 05
Tap to Navigate · Explore Layers
Tap any marker to set it as ▶ Start or ⚑ End. Open Map Layers to toggle what you see: access gates, crossings, parking, detour routes, free viewing spots, and Google Places — including nearby hotels, restaurants, cafés, and bars.
05 of 05
Tips, Map Styles & Offline Use
Tap ❓ Replay Tutorial at the bottom of the menu anytime. Switch between Light, Dark, Satellite, and Hybrid map views. Note: an internet connection is needed for maps and routing — save offline maps in Google Maps before your trip as backup.
